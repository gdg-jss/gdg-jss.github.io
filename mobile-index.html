Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about GDG and how to get involved

What is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google's developer technology.

How can I join GDG?

You can join by attending our events, workshops, and registering through our community portal. No prior experience is required!

What technologies do you cover?

We cover a wide range of Google technologies including Android, Flutter, Firebase, Google Cloud, TensorFlow, and more.

Are the events free?

Yes, all our events, workshops, and study jams are completely free for students to attend.
